HOW SAFE IS WHATSAPP?

WhatsApp utilizes end-to-end encryption to safeguard data while it's being transmitted from the sender to the recipient. This encryption ensures that the messages, calls, and photos sent via the secure messaging app are encrypted during transit. Consequently, only the sender and the intended recipient have the ability to decipher and view the content.Messages that are end-to-end encrypted remain inaccessible to any unauthorized parties while in transit. Through encryption, the message is transformed into unintelligible code, guaranteeing that only the sender and the recipient possess the means to decrypt and access its contents. The same level of security extends to voice and video calls as well as photos shared through WhatsApp. It's important to note that end-to-end encryption is not exclusive to WhatsApp; other messaging platforms such as Telegram and Signal also offer this security feature. However, WhatsApp is among the most widely adopted services providing this robust level of encryption.
